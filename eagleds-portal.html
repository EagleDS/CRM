BELT PROGRESSION
White Belt to Blue Belt — the full Eagle DS pathway
Pathway Overview
Every student follows the same pathway. Every grading is assessed against the same criteria. Sequences 1–4 take a student to Orange Belt. Sequences 5–8 take a student to Blue Belt. Blue Belt qualifies a coach to teach kids. Black Belt qualifies a coach to take adults — content in development.

Sambo · Foundation Pathway
SAMBO
Sambo foundation integrated into the Eagle DS pathway from Orange Belt
Sambo content is integrated into Sequences 7 and 8 and carries through to Blue Belt. Full standalone Sambo syllabus is accessible at the Orange Belt level. See the Sambo Syllabus document for the complete belt progression and technique library.
Eagle DS · Coaching Qualification
40-WEEK COURSE
The full coaching qualification pathway · New weeks published as the course develops
Course Architecture
Weeks 1–7: Foundation — philosophy, standards, fundamentals, coaching kids, operational definitions, event management
Weeks 8–14: E.A.G.L.E Framework — the intellectual spine of the system
Weeks 15–24: Technical delivery — Boxing, Kickboxing, MMA syllabus teaching
Weeks 25–32: Sambo & Taekwondo delivery
Weeks 33–36: Competition coaching — CGS, cornering, athlete preparation
Weeks 37–40: Running a gym — systems, communication, grading events
